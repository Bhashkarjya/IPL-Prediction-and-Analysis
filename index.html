IPL Predictor

A website to provide IPL prediction and thorough analysis of the IPL matches

IPL matches are known for their unexpected twists and turns. It's a platform where talent meets oppurtunity. The Indian Premier League changed the way cricket is played in India and has brought in huge scope for the entertainment industry as well. Twenty20 cricket became phenomenally popular after India won the Twenty20 World Cup in 2007 and the IPL has since then cashed on the craze. From then on, India's involvement with IPL has been rewarding and marvelous.

Get the recent events related to IPL

MI crowned Champions

Mumbai Indians has been crowned the winners of the Dream11 IPL for the record 5th time In the finals, they defeated Delhi Capitals who were making their first appearance in the IPL finals

MSD will be back in IPL 2021

MS Dhoni assures his fans that he will seen playing in the yellow jersey in the next edition of IPL. During the toss, he was asked about whether this will be his last match in the yellow jersey. He replied wittingly, "Definitely Not".

More teams incoming

The BCCI stated that IPL 2021 will see the emergence of one or two more teams. Inside sources, suggest that one of the teams could be a Ahmedabad based franchise.

Rahul gets the Orange Cap

KL Rahul gets the Orange Cap for the year 2020. He was in a tremendous form in this tournament and amassed close to 700 runs at an staggering average of 50+.

Padikkal rise up to expectations

Devdutt Padikkal, RCB's opening batsman stole the show with the bat. He got the Emerging Player Award for his awesome batting display.

Dada made it possible

Sourav Ganguly and his team made sure that people witnessed an awesome IPL. Praises are garnered by Dada and his team from all over the world.
